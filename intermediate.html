Create a Graph in FRED

Goal: Create a graph showing the U.S. unemployment rate over the last 10 years.

Watch the tutorial

Steps

  1. Go to the FRED website.
  2. In the search box type unemployment rate.
  3. Select Civilian Unemployment Rate (UNRATE).
  4. Adjust the date range to the last 10 years.
  5. Click Download to export the graph.
