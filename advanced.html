Analyze Economic Growth in FRED

Goal: Transform GDP data to show percent change and analyze economic growth trends.

Watch the tutorial

Steps

  1. Go to the FRED website.
  2. In the search box type real gdp.
  3. Select Real Gross Domestic Product (GDPC1).
  4. Click Edit Graph above the graph.
  5. In the Units dropdown menu select Percent Change from Year Ago.
  6. Adjust the time range to view economic growth over the last 20–30 years.
  7. Observe periods where GDP growth slowed or declined.
  8. Click Download if you want to export the graph.
